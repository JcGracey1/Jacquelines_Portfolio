Portfolio of Jacqueline Gracey

About Me

Hello, I'm Jacqueline Gracey—a game designer driven by the challenge of transforming intricate problems into captivating gaming experiences. Currently in my final year pursuing a Bachelor’s in Computer Science: Game Design at UC Santa Cruz, I have developed strong skills in C++, Java, and JavaScript, focusing on creating interactive and immersive games. My portfolio features innovative projects like ‘Starcatto’ and ‘Protest Pacer,’ where I've demonstrated my ability to blend technical skill with creative game mechanics. Beyond coding, I stay active with workouts and team sports, which enhance my focus and collaborative spirit—traits I bring to every project. Eager to contribute to pioneering gaming projects, I am on the lookout for opportunities that challenge traditional gameplay and align with my passion for game development.

Projects

  • Starcatto

This project promotes the exploration of audio and visuals in a space themed canvas. Connect different stars to explore new combinations of sounds. Press on a planet to add a drum beat.
Project members: Jacqueline Gracey, Jose Nadales, Jim Lee, Thanyared Wong, Brent Chou, Fernando Alcazar

  • Protest Pacer

Protest pacer was my first game project completed at UCSC. It is a reimagining of Snake; functions added include blocking a car will give the player more points, or using the space bar as a speed boost.
Project members: Jacqueline Gracey, Jarod Spangler, Michael Campanile
